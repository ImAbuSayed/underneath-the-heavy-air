[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
[Breathy]
Pulling you closer as the midnight turns to rust
Breathing in the warmth of this quiet kind of trust
Your shadows dancing slow against the amber glow
There is nowhere else I ever want to go

[Verse 1]
[Intimate]
The engine cools outside in the gravel and the rain
The porch light flickers softly against the window pane
I watch the way you move across the dimly lit room
Getting lost entirely inside your sweet perfume

[Pre-Chorus]
[Building anticipation]
I feel your fingers drawing circles on my skin
Whispering the places where the both of us begin
The world outside is fading, it is only me and you
Falling through the gravity, breaking into something new

[Chorus]
[Anthemic peak]
Underneath the heavy air, catching every breath you leave
Taking every part of you, giving everything you need
Losing all the boundaries in the middle of the night
Underneath the heavy air, burning in the sudden light

[Post-Chorus]
[Smooth, harmonized]
(We are drifting in the warm glow)
Never letting this moment go

[Verse 2]
[Tender longing]
Tasting the sweetness of the words you leave unsaid
A beautiful surrender swimming softly in my head
Every gentle motion is a rhythm that I know
Melting in the current, taking everything so slow

[Pre-Chorus 2]
[Intense anticipation]
I feel your fingers drawing circles on my skin

Falling through the gravity, breaking into something new

[Chorus]
[Intense]
Underneath the heavy air, catching every breath you leave

[Post-Chorus 2]
[Smooth, echoed]
(We are drifting in the warm glow)
Never letting this moment go

[Bridge]
[Reflective and soulful]
Running from the morning, holding steady in the dark
A quiet kind of fever leaving a permanent mark
Tangled in the moment taking over everywhere
Losing our defenses in the middle of the stare
Even Saeed surrenders, breathing in this heavy air
Never looking backward, we are floating on a spark

[Spoken]
[Whispered passion]

We are exactly where we are supposed to be

[Final Chorus]
[Highest emotional peak]
